Detailed Product Information - Locator Device

The locator device helps **prevent kidnapping** through its real-time location tracking, emergency communication, and evidence provision in dangerous situations. Parents can track their children's location, ensure their safety, and communicate when necessary. The device can also record ambient sound and alert if the child leaves a safe zone or presses the SOS button.

Key Benefits of the Locator Device for Kidnap Prevention:

  • Accurate Location Tracking: Helps parents know the child's real-time location via GPS and LBS.
  • Two-Way Communication: Allows two-way calls with a pre-set contact list.
  • SOS Alert: The child presses the SOS button to instantly send an alert and location.
  • Safe Zone Setup (Geo-fencing): Sends alerts if the child leaves a designated safe area.
  • **Environmental Listening:** Helps parents assess the level of danger.
  • **Assistance for Lost Children:** Easy to locate and contact.
  • **Develops Independence:** Children feel safe and confident to explore.
